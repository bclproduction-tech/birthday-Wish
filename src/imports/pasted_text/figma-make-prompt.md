# Figma Make Prompt

Design and build a **luxury cinematic single-page web application** that feels like a timeless digital love letter.

The design should feel like something created by a high-end creative agency—minimal, elegant, emotional, and unforgettable.

## Theme

This website is a birthday gift dedicated to **Almas Abdalla**, lovingly known as **Hawa**.

She is also called **Luna**, a poetic name that beautifully complements mine, **Alpha**.

We have shared a journey together since **2018**.

Although life has become uncertain and we may have drifted apart, this website should never feel desperate or guilty. Instead, it should communicate gratitude, admiration, hope, and enduring love.

The overall emotional tone should be:

* nostalgic
* peaceful
* elegant
* deeply romantic
* mature
* luxurious
* cinematic

Think:

> "A memory preserved in light."

Not:

> "Please come back."

# Overall Feeling

This should feel like a premium interactive film rather than a website.

Visitors should experience calm, warmth, nostalgia, admiration, and beauty. Every detail—from spacing and typography to animation and transitions—should reflect refined craftsmanship. The design should celebrate cherished memories and a heartfelt birthday while leaving space for hope, without ever feeling pleading or sorrowful.

The finished result should be comparable in quality to an award-winning portfolio or luxury brand microsite, with an emotional storytelling experience that is unforgettable.
